Artificial intelligence (AI) Superintelligence
is the type of “intelligence” you can put into a device to perform a specific task. Google Translate is AI. Self-driven cars are AI. This kind of intelligence already exists is a kind of intelligence that vastly outperforms human (individual) intelligence. This kind of intelligence is just hypothetical at the moment. We will need it to investigate areas of scientific research that are beyond our reach (usually because there are too many parameters to keep under control)
